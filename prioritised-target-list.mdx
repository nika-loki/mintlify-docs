Create companies target list views for your team

<Card title="Instructions">
  1. Go to **Companies** on the sidebar
  2. Click on **Edit columns** on the top right of the table (create a new view if you prefer)
  3. Search `SalesDuo` in the search bar
  4. Add **SalesDuo Account Signals**, **SalesDuo Account Score**, **SalesDuo Account Signal Last Refreshed** to selected columns (Ideally, move to  top)
  5. For prioritisation, sort by **SalesDuo Account Score**, descending, from highest to lowest
  6. Hit <Icon icon="floppy-disk" /> **Save** in the top right of the table

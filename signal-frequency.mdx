Learn how to configure scheduled runs so that your accounts get automatically re-prioritised

<Card title="Scheduling Runs">
  <Frame>
    ![Image](/images/image-4.png)
  </Frame>

  You are able to set your custom research frequency to:

  - Scout (Monthly)
  - Ranger (Weekly) → Most Popular
  - Hunter (3 Days)
  - Ultimate (Daily)

  <Note>
    The most common strategy is to re-prioritise <u>weekly</u>. 

    Only choose Scout (monthly) if you are willing to sacrifice results for saving cost.
  </Note>
</Card>

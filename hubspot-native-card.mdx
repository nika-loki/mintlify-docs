<Card title="Instructions">
  1. Go to <Icon icon="gear-complex" /> **Settings** at the top bar
  2. Scroll down on the left sidebar to find **Data Management** \> **Objects \> Companies**
  3. In the Companies pane, click on **Record Customization**
  4. Click into **Default view** (you can choose to create a new team view)
  5. Under the **Edit Layout** tab in Company record page, hover between the section and click on **Add Card**
  6. In the side panel, click on **Card library** tab \> search **SalesDuo Signals**\> click **\+Add card**

  <Tip>
    You can **repeat step 2-4** to add **SalesDuo Signal Card** to <u>Contacts</u> view and <u>Deals</u> view
  </Tip>
